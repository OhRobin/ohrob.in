1. Service Description

What CopyPasta Does

CopyPasta is a macOS desktop application that:

  • Connects to your Gmail account via OAuth2 authentication
  • Monitors your Gmail inbox for unread emails containing 2FA verification codes
  • Automatically extracts 4-8 digit verification codes using pattern recognition
  • Copies detected codes to your macOS clipboard
  • Marks processed emails as read to prevent duplicate processing
  • Displays notifications when codes are found and copied

What CopyPasta Does NOT Do

  • Store, transmit, or share your email content with third parties
  • Access emails older than 10 minutes
  • Modify, delete, or send emails from your account
  • Access Gmail contacts, drafts, or other data beyond inbox messages
  • Work with email providers other than Gmail
